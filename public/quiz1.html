第二題：布林代數與卡諾圖

(a) 最簡 SOP 卡諾圖化簡

$$ F = \Sigma(1, 2, 3, 5, 7) = z + x'y $$

(b) SOP 與 POS 轉換：

$$ F = x'y + y'z + xz = \Sigma(1, 2, 3, 5, 7) = \Pi(0, 4, 6) $$

$$ F' = \Sigma(0, 4, 6) = \Pi(1, 2, 3, 5, 7) $$

第三題：邏輯閘電路實作

基礎函數卡諾圖推導 \( f(A,B,C) = \Sigma(0, 2, 3, 4, 6) \)

(1) 圈選 1 得到 SOP

$$ f = C' + A'B $$

(2) 圈選 0 得到 POS

\( f' = AC + B'C \)

\( f = (f')' = (AC + B'C)' \)

$$ \Rightarrow f = (A'+C')(B+C') $$

(a) AND-OR

\( f = A'B + C' \)

(b) OR-AND

\( f = (A'+C')(B+C') \)

(c) NAND-NAND

\( f = ((A'B)' \cdot C)' = A'B + C' \)

(d) NOR-NOR

\( f = ((A'+C')' + (B+C')')' \)

(e) NOR-OR

\( f = (A+B')' + C' = A'B + C' \)

(f) NAND-AND

\( f = (AC)' \cdot (B'C)' \)

(g) OR-NAND

\( f = ((A+B') \cdot C)' \)

(h) AND-NOR

\( f = (AC + B'C)' \)
